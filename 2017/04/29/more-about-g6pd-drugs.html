More About G6PD Drugs

Bergwolf G6PD浩宇

The common over-the-counter treatments for fever, pain, nasal congestion and cough — acetaminophen (Tylenol, 扑热息痛,醋氨酚,对乙酰氨基酚), ibuprofen (Advil, 布洛芬,异丁苯丙酸), pseudoephedrine (Sudafed, 伪麻黄硷), and guaifenesin (Robitussin, 愈美,愈创甘油醚，愈创木酚甘油醚) — are usually safe at normal therapeutic doses for children with G6PD deficiency. People with G6PD deficiency should avoid medicines that contain aspirin (salicylic acid).

Common Over-the-Counter Medications to AVOID

  • Anacin (阿纳辛)
  • Aspirin (阿司匹林)
  • Bufferin (心湿林,巴非林)
  • Ecotrin (阿司匹林制剂)
  • Empirin (安匹林)
  • Excedrin (埃克塞德林)
  • Pepto Bismol (水杨酸铋, 消化液,止泻药)

Common Prescription drugs to AVOID

Antimalarials:

  • Chloroquine (Aralen)
  • Mefloquine (Lariam)
  • Pamaquine
  • Primaquine
  • Quinidine
  • Quinine

Sulfonamides and Sulfones:

  • Dapsone
  • Furosemide (Lasix)
  • Sulfacetamide (Sulamyd)
  • Sulfamethoxazole (Bactrim, Septra)
  • Sulfanilamide
  • Sulfasalazine (Azulfidine)
  • Sulfisoxazole (Gantrisin)

Nitrofurans

  • Nitrofurantoin (Furadantin, Macrobid, Macrodantin)

Quinolones and Fluoroquinolones:

  • Ciprofloxacin (Cipro)
  • Levofloxacin (Levaquin)
  • Moxifloxacin (Avelox, Vigamox)
  • Norfloxacin (Noroxin)

Other things to AVOID

  • Acetylphenylhydrazine (乙酰苯肼)
  • Beta-Naphthol (β-萘酚)
  • Chloramphenicol (氯霉素)
  • Dimercaprol (二巯基丙醇,简称BAL,常用作砷/汞等中毒的解毒剂)
  • Fava beans (蚕豆)
  • Glyburide (格列本脲,优降糖,用于治疗糖尿病)
  • Henna (印度海娜,音译名,India Henna,在印度又名指甲花,凤仙花,散沫花)
  • Menthol (薄荷醇,薄荷脑)
  • Methylene Blue (亚甲蓝,染料)
  • Moth balls (萘,樟脑球)
  • Nitrofurazone (呋喃西林,呋喃新,呋喃星,硝基呋喃腙,消毒防腐药，外用于治疗创伤,烧伤,化脓性皮炎,中耳炎,泪囊炎,阴道炎,膀胱冲洗,褥疮等)
  • Penicillamine (青霉胺,青霉素的代谢产物,可通过水解青霉素制备,重金属中毒解毒剂,治疗风湿)
  • Phenazopyridine (非那吡啶,尿通宁,麻醉剂)
  • Phenylhydrazine (苯肼,一种用作检测糖和醛类的化学试剂)
  • Probenecid (丙磺舒,羧苯磺丙胺,一种作用于肾管功能的药物)
  • Rasburicase (拉布立酶,安万特,Elitek)
  • Tolbutamide (甲苯磺丁脲,甲糖宁,一种治糖尿病药,山地酶,Orinase)
  • Toluidine Blue (甲苯胺蓝,染料)
